Starter practice modes

Greetings

Practise hello, how are you, thank you and goodbye in a two-line conversation.

Family

Introduce mother, father, siblings and home using simple spoken sentences.

Food

Role-play asking for water, tea, price and the bill.

Travel

Ask where the bathroom, hotel, bus stop and shop are.

Questions

Drill what, where, who, when, why, how and how much.

Mock test

Run a gentle starter mock test without claiming official certification.

Teacher-approved limits
