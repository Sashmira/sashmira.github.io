How to use the library

  1. Read one story slowly in Roman Malayalam.
  2. Check the English meaning and underline five useful words.
  3. Read the story aloud twice and record one voice note.
  4. Answer the quiz questions without looking.
  5. Use the AI tutor prompt to practise a short conversation.
