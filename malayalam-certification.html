Assessment

What a useful Malayalam certificate should test

A real certificate should measure practical language ability: listening, speaking, pronunciation, vocabulary, sentence building, interaction and cultural context.

  • Listening comprehension with natural Malayalam clips.
  • Spoken response to everyday prompts.
  • Pronunciation and rhythm checks.
  • Vocabulary in context, not isolated lists.
  • Conversation role play for family, travel or work.
  • Optional reading and writing track.

FAQ

Common questions

Can I take the certification today?

The full certification system is a future network product. This page sets the public foundation and preparation pathway.

Who is certification for?

Learners, tutors, translators, schools and employers who need a clearer way to prove Malayalam ability.

Will it include reading and writing?

The core path should focus on speaking first, with optional reading and writing tracks for learners who need script.

Prepare before certification opens.

Build your speaking base first. Certification should prove real skill, not only memorized vocabulary.
