Family calls

Malayalam conversation practice for family calls

Many learners want Malayalam because of family. A good family-call practice plan starts with small, warm lines you can actually use.

Open family learning path

Simple family-call flow

Greeting: Start with hello, how are you and respectful address.
Update: Say where you are, what you ate, how work or school is going.
Question: Ask about health, home, food, plans or the children.
Feeling: Add happy, tired, busy, worried or missing you.
Ending: Close politely with see you, call later or take care.

Practice scenes

Grandparent call

Use respectful tone, health questions and short replies.

Child practice

Keep it playful with names, family words and easy yes/no questions.
