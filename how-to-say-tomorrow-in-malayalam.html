How to Say "Tomorrow" in Malayalam

Casual, everyday Malayalam. By Reshmi R Nair, Malayalam Linguist.

നാളെ
Naale — Tomorrow

In short: "Tomorrow" in Malayalam is നാളെ (naale). "Today" is ഇന്ന് (innu) and "yesterday" is ഇന്നലെ (innale).

Time words around "tomorrow"

MalayalamRomanisedEnglish
നാളെNaaleTomorrow
ഇന്ന്InnuToday

മറ്റന്നാള്MattannaalDay after tomorrow

Example sentences

നാളെ കാണാം.Naale kaanaam.See you tomorrow.
നാളെ ഞാൻ വരാം.Naale njaan varaam.I'll come tomorrow.

Frequently asked questions

How do you say tomorrow in Malayalam?

Tomorrow is naale (നാളെ).

How do you say "see you tomorrow"?

Naale kaanaam (നാളെ കാണാം).

How do you say today and yesterday?

Today is innu (ഇന്ന്); yesterday is innale (ഇന്നലെ).
