The Method

The S.L.A.M.™ Method — speak from day one.

S.L.A.M.™ — my Spoken Language Architecture for Malayalam — is a structured, spoken-first system built for international learners of every age. Spoken Malayalam is a motor and listening skill, not a memory test. You learn in simplified Roman Malayalam (no script needed to start) and speak from your very first lesson.

01

M.A.P.™ Framework

Mouth · Audio · Pattern. Correct pronunciation and mouth movement, listening accuracy and rhythm, and reusable Malayalam sentence patterns — the building blocks of real speech.

02

A.R.T.™ Framework

Articulation · Reception · Transformation. Clear, confident speaking, natural understanding of fast everyday Malayalam, and a steady drop in translating from English — until Malayalam comes automatically.

03

English-Maximum Design

Every concept anchored in clear English explanation with inclusive, easy-to-read formatting — built for international learners.

Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to learn the Malayalam script first?
No. You start speaking immediately using RRN Manglish, a romanization system that captures every Malayalam sound accurately in Latin letters. The script can come later, if and when you want it.
Are Malayalam lessons taught in English?
Yes. Every explanation is in clear, fluent English. The method is designed English-maximum specifically for international learners, so nothing gets lost in translation.
Can a complete beginner learn Malayalam online?
Absolutely. Most learners start from zero. The pattern-based method is built precisely for adult beginners who want logic, not memorisation.
What time zones do you teach Malayalam in?
All of them. Lessons run 5:00 AM to 11:30 PM IST daily, serving learners in Europe, North America, Australia and the Middle East at their local morning, afternoon or evening.
How long does it take to learn to speak Malayalam?
With one or two lessons a week plus light practice, most learners hold simple conversations within 3 to 4 months and comfortable family-level chat within a year. The pattern method compounds fast.
Do you teach Malayalam through Hindi?
Yes. Alongside English-medium lessons for the international diaspora, practical spoken Malayalam is also taught through Hindi for learners in India, including professionals and healthcare workers living in Kerala.
Can I learn Malayalam from the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany or the Gulf?
Yes — these are exactly the regions I teach. Lessons are 100% online and scheduled in your local time, so learners in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, across Europe and the Middle East all study comfortably from home.
Can I try a first lesson before committing?
Lessons are paid, one-to-one and fully focused on you. You can book a single first lesson to experience the method and receive a personalised learning plan — with no long-term commitment required.
How much do lessons cost?
It varies based on each student — your level, goals and the lesson package you choose. Message me for a quick personalised quote. Pay securely by PayPal or card (worldwide), or Google Pay / UPI (India).
What do I need for an online Malayalam lesson?
We use Google Meet, Zoom or Skype — whichever you prefer. All you need is a phone, tablet or laptop with internet; I send you the link. Nothing to buy or install beyond a free video app.
Do you teach children?
Yes — I teach all ages, from children as young as 5 right up to adults of 80. Lessons are tailored to each learner's age, level and goals; children get patient, playful, spoken-first sessions.
How long is each lesson?
Each lesson is 45 or 60 minutes — you choose what suits you best. Multi-lesson packages are also available.
What is your cancellation & rescheduling policy?
Please reschedule or cancel at least 24 hours before your lesson — there is no charge and we will simply find a new time. For cancellations or no-shows with less than 24 hours' notice, the lesson is charged in full, out of fairness to the time reserved for you.
How do I book and pay?
Book through iTalki, Preply, Verbling, AmazingTalker or Superprof, or directly on this site — pay by PayPal (worldwide) or Google Pay / UPI (India). Book your first lesson any time.
