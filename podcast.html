Frequently asked questions

How do I subscribe to the podcast?

Copy the feed address speakmalayalam.com/podcast.xml into any podcast app's 'add show by URL' option. Spotify and Apple directory listings are coming.

Are episodes real colloquial Malayalam?

Yes — every episode is everyday spoken Malayalam, the register used at home and in shops, never textbook-formal.

How long are episodes?

Seconds to a minute — designed for repetition. Hear it, say it, use it the same day.

Will there be more episodes?

Yes — new phrases and full dialogues are added regularly from the growing audio library.
