Marketplace model

Learners need people. People need trust.

The marketplace should not be a random directory. It should connect need, proof and outcomes: learners who need help, professionals who can help, and credentials that make trust easier.

  • Learners request tutors by goal and time zone.
  • Tutors list skills, languages, availability and proof.
  • Employers post Malayalam work requirements.
  • Candidates show certification, samples and experience.
  • SpeakMalayalam keeps the category organized.
