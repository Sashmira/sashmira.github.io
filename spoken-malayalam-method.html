The method

Learn spoken Malayalam through patterns, not memorising

The SpeakMalayalam method starts with real-life speech: useful sentences, sound practice, English explanations and repeatable patterns that help a beginner speak before they feel buried in rules.

Use the course path

The five-part method

Start with speaking goals. Family calls, travel, daily conversation, kids at home or workplace needs decide the first lesson path.
Use romanised Malayalam carefully. Beginners can start speaking before reading Malayalam script, while pronunciation notes keep the sound accurate.
Teach sentence patterns. Learners build sentences from reusable blocks instead of memorising isolated word lists.
Correct sound and confidence. Pronunciation, listening and response speed matter because the goal is live conversation.
Connect every lesson to practice. Articles, worksheets, flashcards, quizzes, AI tutor prompts, course lessons and human feedback support the same topic.

What this beats

Script-first overwhelm

Script is valuable, but many conversation learners need spoken confidence first.

Random phrase collecting

Useful phrases matter more when they are connected to patterns and practice.

App-only learning

Apps can help habit, but serious speaking usually needs feedback and real examples.
