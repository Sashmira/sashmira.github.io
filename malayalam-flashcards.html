How flashcards fit the network

  1. Find a word in the dictionary or lesson.
  2. Review it as a flashcard until recall is fast.
  3. Use the word in a worksheet or story prompt.
  4. Practise aloud with the AI vocabulary drill.
  5. Use human feedback for pronunciation, placement and certification readiness.
