Malayalam Lessons Online: Start Speaking Step by Step

A structured lesson library for beginners who want real colloquial Malayalam for family, Kerala travel, daily life and conversation.

Use this page as your Malayalam lesson map. Start with beginner basics, then move into pronunciation, phrases, grammar, conversation and real situations. Every lesson should lead you to the next skill, not leave you with random words.

Recommended path: Start with Malayalam for beginners, then follow the 30-day course, practise with the audio phrasebook, and use live lessons when you need correction.
