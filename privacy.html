Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · RRN Colloquial Malayalam (Dr. Reshmi R Nair)

Your privacy matters. This page explains, in plain language, what information this website collects, why, and the choices you have. We keep it simple because we collect very little.

Who we are

This website (sashmira.github.io) is run by Dr. Reshmi R Nair, an independent Malayalam, English and Hindi language tutor. For any privacy question, contact dr.reshmi.linguist@gmail.com.

What information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us through a form on this site:

  • Your name and email address — when you request the free phrasebook or submit a booking/enquiry.
  • Optional details you type — such as your country, the language you want to learn, and your message.

We do not run advertising trackers, and the site itself does not set marketing cookies.

Why we use it

Only to: send you the free resource you asked for, reply to your enquiry, and arrange lessons. The legal basis is your consent, given when you submit the form. We never sell or rent your details to anyone.

The services we rely on

  • FormSubmit — delivers your form message to our email inbox.
  • PayPal and UPI providers — process payments securely; we never see or store your card details.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website (serves the pages).
  • Google Fonts — loads the fonts you see.

Each of these has its own privacy policy and handles data under its own terms.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to see, correct, or delete the information you have shared. Just email dr.reshmi.linguist@gmail.com and we will act promptly. If you are in the EU/UK, these are your rights under GDPR; we are happy to honour them for everyone.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry and student details only as long as needed to help you, and delete them on request.

Children

We teach learners of all ages, including children, but lessons for minors are arranged with a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from a child without parental involvement.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email dr.reshmi.linguist@gmail.com. Back to home.
