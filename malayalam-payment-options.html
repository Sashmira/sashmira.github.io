Payment routes to prepare

India learners

UPI or Razorpay-style payment links may fit India-first lessons, audio packs, books and short courses once the account and product delivery are tested.

International learners

PayPal, Payhip, Stripe-style checkout or manual invoice routes may fit learners in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Europe and the Gulf.

Organizations

Corporate or school work should start with an inquiry, scope, schedule and quote before any payment link is shared.

Safe checkout rule

No fake checkout, no rushed payment.
Payment links should be added only after the exact product, delivery method, refund/support wording and payment provider are tested. This keeps SpeakMalayalam simple, static, low-risk and easier to maintain.
