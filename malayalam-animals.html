Malayalam Words for Animals

By Dr. Reshmi R Nair, PhD · Updated June 2026

Animal words are a favourite — especially for children learning Malayalam. Here are the most common ones.

EnglishMalayalamPronunciation
Dogനായnaaya
Catപൂച്ചpoocha
Cowപശുpashu
Elephantആനaana
Horseകുതിരkuthira
Lionസിംഹംsimham
Tigerപുലിpuli
Birdപക്ഷിpakshi
Fishമീൻmeen
Snakeപാമ്പ്paambu
Monkeyകുരങ്ങ്kurangu
Goatആട്aadu

How to practise

  • Say each word aloud — sound first.
  • Use them in tiny sentences with words from common words.
  • Test yourself on the free flashcards & quiz.
