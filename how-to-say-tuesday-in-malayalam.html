How to Say "Tuesday" in Malayalam

Casual, everyday Malayalam. By Reshmi R Nair, Malayalam Linguist.

ചൊവ്വാഴ്ച
Chovvaazhcha — Tuesday

In short: "Tuesday" in Malayalam is ചൊവ്വാഴ്ച (chovvaazhcha). The day-name on its own is chovva; aazhcha (ആഴ്ച) means "week", and is added to each day.

Example sentences

ഇന്ന് ചൊവ്വാഴ്ച ആണ്.Innu chovvaazhcha aanu.Today is Tuesday.
ചൊവ്വാഴ്ച കാണാം.Chovvaazhcha kaanaam.See you on Tuesday.

Frequently asked questions

How do you say Tuesday in Malayalam?

Tuesday is chovvaazhcha (ചൊവ്വാഴ്ച); the day-name alone is chovva.

What does aazhcha mean?

Aazhcha (ആഴ്ച) means "week", added to each day-name.

How do you say "today is Tuesday"?

Innu chovvaazhcha aanu (ഇന്ന് ചൊവ്വാഴ്ച ആണ്).
