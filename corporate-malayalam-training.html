Program design

Training should match the workplace

A useful corporate program starts with the real situations people face. The curriculum should be short, spoken-first, role-play heavy and supported by printable phrase sheets.

  • Needs audit by role and learner level.
  • Phrase bank for the exact work situation.
  • Short live sessions or recorded modules.
  • Role plays and pronunciation correction.
  • Team worksheets and post-session practice.
  • Optional certification readiness path.

FAQ

Common questions

Is corporate Malayalam training only for companies in Kerala?

No. It can help global teams, schools, healthcare groups, customer service teams and organizations serving Malayali communities anywhere.

Can training be customized?

Yes. Corporate training should be built around the exact phrases, situations and outcomes the team needs.

Can this connect to certification?

Yes. Future certification can provide a clearer proof layer for team training outcomes.

Build a practical Malayalam training path for your team.

Start with goals, learner level and real situations. Then build a short course around the Malayalam your team actually needs.
