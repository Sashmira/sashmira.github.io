Press note

SpeakMalayalam is preparing a Malayalam learning book library for adult learners, children, parents, script learners and heritage families. The project connects books with companion pages, flashcards, parent routines, audio planning, tutor support and practical learning paths so Malayalam learners are not forced to rely on scattered resources.
