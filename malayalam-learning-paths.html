What a serious Malayalam learning ecosystem should include

A strong learner experience should combine free discovery, structured course pages, human tutor routes, app-style practice, review tools, worksheets, AI drills, community, certification pathways and future marketplace/jobs routes.

SpeakMalayalam is being built as that full network. Some parts are live now, and some are queued locally for guarded deployment. The page keeps claims honest and routes learners to the strongest available next step.

Important honesty notes

  • Native app-store availability is not claimed until store review passes.
  • Certification is a pathway unless a specific issued credential is verified.
  • AI practice is a support tool, not a replacement for human correction.
  • Payment routes should stay separate until provider setup and product proofing pass.
