Use the correct website address

Copy and paste this exact address into the browser address bar:

https://speakmalayalam.com/

The correct spelling is speakmalayalam.com with the full word malayalam.

Common mistake: speakmalayalm.com is missing the second a in Malayalam. That wrong address can make a healthy website look unavailable.

Fast recovery

  • Remove any final period, comma, bracket or space after the link.

If a student abroad cannot open the link

  1. Ask for the exact address they typed or clicked.
  2. Ask for a screenshot of the browser error.
  3. Ask whether they are in the UK, USA, Canada, Europe, the Gulf, Australia or another country.
  4. Ask whether they used home Wi-Fi, mobile data, school Wi-Fi or workplace Wi-Fi.
  5. Send them the access help page and the homepage link.
