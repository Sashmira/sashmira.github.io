Malayalam online course syllabus: what you should learn first

A good Malayalam online course should not jump randomly from grammar to vocabulary. It should build from sound to words to patterns to real conversation.

View course path

Beginner syllabus

Module 1: Sounds and greetings. Learn pronunciation, hello, how are you, thank you and simple replies.
Module 2: Family and home. Learn family words, respectful address, home vocabulary and first family-call scripts.
Module 3: Food and daily life. Practise water, rice, tea, likes, wants, requests and meal questions.
Module 4: Questions and verbs. Use what, where, when, who, come, go, eat, drink, want, know and speak.
Module 5: Conversation practice. Build short dialogues for calls, visits, shopping, travel and tutor role-play.
Module 6: Reading and certification path. Add Malayalam script, review progress, and prepare for starter certification.

What this syllabus should connect to

Each course module should feed the wider network: free lessons, worksheets, AI practice, tutor correction, starter testing and paid paths.
