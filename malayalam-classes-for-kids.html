Frequently asked questions

What age can my child start Malayalam classes?

From age 5. Lessons for young children are shorter, playful and song-based; older kids and teens follow a faster conversational track.

My child understands Malayalam but won't speak it. Can you help?

Yes — this is the most common starting point for heritage kids. Spoken-first, judgement-free practice converts passive understanding into confident speaking surprisingly fast.

Do kids need to learn the Malayalam script?

Not to start. Speaking comes first in easy Romanised Malayalam; script reading is added when your child is ready, if you wish.

Can siblings learn together?

Yes — family rates and sibling arrangements are available. Many families alternate one-to-one lessons between children.
