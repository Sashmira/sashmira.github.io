How to compare the options fairly

School-style structure: Helpful for learners who want a formal, class-like progression and script emphasis.
Spoken-first practice: Helpful for beginners who need phrases, correction and confidence in real conversations.
Human feedback: Important if pronunciation, hesitation or word choice are the main problems.
Long-term path: Stronger when lessons connect to books, worksheets, membership, certification and continued practice.
