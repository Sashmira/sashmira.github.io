Why most learners get stuck

Many beginners are pushed into script, grammar labels or random word lists before they can say anything useful. A learner who wants to speak Malayalam with family needs a different first step: useful sentences, sound practice, sentence patterns, listening and real correction.

That is why SpeakMalayalam is being built around a spoken-first ladder: learn, practice, certify, teach and work.
