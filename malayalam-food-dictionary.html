Roman MalayalamMeaningExampleEnglish

chaayateaChaaya undo?Do you have tea?
kaapicoffeeKaapi venam.I want coffee.
palmilkPal kudikkumo?Will you drink milk?

meenfishMeen curry venam.I want fish curry.
