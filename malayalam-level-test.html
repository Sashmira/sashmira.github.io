Twelve quick questions, two minutes, instant result — find out exactly where your Malayalam stands and which path fits you. No sign-up needed.

Question 1 of 12

Why test your level?

Most learners either underestimate themselves (heritage listeners who understand everything but freeze when speaking) or start with material that’s too advanced and quit. Two minutes of honest measurement saves months of wrong-level studying — and tells you exactly which free resource or course fits you today.

Frequently asked questions

Is this Malayalam level test free?

Completely free, no sign-up — answer 12 questions and get an instant result with a personalised recommendation.

What do the results mean?

New Explorer means start from the beginning; Confident Starter means you understand basics and should focus on speaking; Hidden Speaker means strong passive knowledge needing conversational polish.

I understand Malayalam but can't speak it — what will I score?

Usually Hidden Speaker — the classic heritage-learner profile. The recommended path converts your passive understanding into confident speech.

How accurate is a 12-question test?

It places you in the right starting band. For a precise placement, book a speaking & pronunciation assessment with Reshmi.
