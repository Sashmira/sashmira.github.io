Who should join

Adult learners

Especially learners who understand Malayalam but struggle to answer back.

Parents

Families teaching Malayalam to children ages 3 to 13 at home.

Teachers and tutors

Malayalam educators who can judge sequence, usefulness and practice quality.

Native speakers

Native Malayalam speakers who can catch wording, Manglish and authenticity issues.

Review rules

Good

Advance copy for honest feedback. Private notes welcome. Public review optional. Reader writes in their own words.

Not allowed

No gift cards, payment, forced reviews, scripted reviews, five-star requests, or rewards for posting a review.

What feedback helps most

Usefulness

Who is this book best for? What part felt most useful in real life?

Clarity

Where did the learner feel confused, overloaded or under-supported?

Language quality

Were any Malayalam, Manglish or English explanations awkward?

Recommendation

Would you recommend it to a parent, adult beginner, school, tutor or community?
