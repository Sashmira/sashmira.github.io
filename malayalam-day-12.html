Day 12 — The question words

20 minutes · real spoken Malayalam · with audio

40% of the course behind you

🎥 5 words + eyebrows = every question you’ll ever need.

Five little words unlock every conversation: what, where, when, how, who. Master these and you can be curious in Malayalam forever.

Today’s phrases

Say itScriptMeaning
Entha?എന്താ?What?
Evide?എവിടെ?Where?
Eppo?എപ്പോ?When?
Engane?എങ്ങനെ?How?
Aaraa?ആരാ?Who is it?
Enthukondu?എന്തുകൊണ്ട്?Why?
🔊 Hear it spoken — tap play, repeat out loud
💡 The one-word conversation

These work alone! Someone shows you something: 'Entha?' Someone's going somewhere: 'Evide?' Phone rings: 'Aaraa?' Malayalis run entire conversations on single question words plus eyebrows. The eyebrows are mandatory.

Mini dialogue — act it out

Friend: Njaan pokuvaa!
I'm off!

You: Evide?
Where?

Friend: Kalyanam!
A wedding!

You: Aarude?
Whose?

Friend: Ariyilla, amma paranju varaan!
No idea, mom said come!

Today’s task

🎯 One-word-question game: reply to everything today (in your head) with the right Malayalam question word. Someone mentions lunch? 'Evide?' A name? 'Aaraa?'
