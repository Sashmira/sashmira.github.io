Why a spoken-first path helps

A child or adult who hears Malayalam at home may still freeze when asked to speak. The first goal is not perfection. The first goal is one useful reply, then one small conversation, then a repeatable habit.

SpeakMalayalam connects family lessons to worksheets, kids books, practice challenges, tutor feedback and future certification paths so family learning can grow into a real Malayalam journey.
