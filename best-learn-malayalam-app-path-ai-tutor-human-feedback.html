The strongest app-supported path

Step 1: Daily app practice. Use short lessons, review loops, word of the day and simple quizzes.
Step 2: AI tutor prompts. Turn each topic into a short speaking or reply practice session.
Step 3: Worksheets. Move from recognition to writing, matching, speaking and mini-dialogues.
Step 4: Human feedback. Use tutor correction when pronunciation, hesitation or natural phrasing matters.
