Frequently asked questions

How do you say thank you in Malayalam?

In casual spoken Malayalam, thank you is "nandi" (നന്ദി).

How do you say I love you in Malayalam?

The full phrase is "njaan ninne snehikkunnu" (ഞാൻ നിന്നെ സ്നേഹിക്കുന്നു); casually, "enikku ninne ishtamaanu".

How do you say how are you in Malayalam?

You say "Sukhamaano?" (സുഖമാണോ?) - literally "are you well?".

Are these casual or formal Malayalam?

Every phrase here is everyday casual spoken Malayalam, the way people really talk in Kerala - not formal textbook Malayalam.
