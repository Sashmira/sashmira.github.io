How to Say "How Far Is It?" in Malayalam

Casual, everyday Malayalam — not textbook. By Dr. Reshmi R Nair, PhD.

എത്ര ദൂരം ഉണ്ട്?
Ethra dooram undu? — How far is it?

▶ Hear it spoken by a native voice.

In short: To ask "how far is it?", say എത്ര ദൂരം ഉണ്ട്? (ethra dooram undu?). To check if it's walkable: നടന്നു പോകാമോ? (nadannu pokaamo?). A common reply: അധികം ദൂരമല്ല (adhikam dooramalla), "not far."

When & how to use it

Dooram means "distance." Pair ethra dooram undu? with nadannu pokaamo? to plan whether to walk or take an auto.

How do you ask how far is it in Malayalam?

"Distance" is ദൂരം (dooram).
