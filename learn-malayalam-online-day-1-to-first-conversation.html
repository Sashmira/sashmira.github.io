Learn Malayalam online: best path from Day 1 to first conversation

The best online Malayalam path is not random videos. It is a sequence: sound, words, sentence frames, listening, correction and real conversation.

Open the beginner roadmap

From Day 1 to conversation

Day 1: Learn greetings and one honest beginner line: I know a little Malayalam.
Days 2-3: Add family, food, water, tea, where and what.
Days 4-7: Practise short patterns with audio and question words.
Week 2: Use tutor, AI or voice-note correction for your first real conversation.

What online learning should include

Lessons

Short lessons that move from phrases to patterns.

Practice

Audio, quizzes, worksheets and speaking prompts.

Correction

Tutor or AI feedback so mistakes do not become habits.
