How a candidate becomes a dictionary entry

1. Request

A learner asks for a word or phrase from real-life use.

2. Native review

A reviewer checks meaning, romanization, script, example use and duplicate status.

3. Publish

Only approved entries become word pages connected to lessons, flashcards, worksheets and AI tutor prompts.
