Five useful starter patterns

I want... Use it for water, tea, help, class, ticket, food and practice.
Where is...? Use it for home, shop, room, station, bus stop and temple.
I am going... Use it for home, school, work, Kerala, class and restaurant.
I know a little... Use it to keep conversations friendly while you are learning.
Please repeat... Use it when Malayalam feels too fast.

Why patterns beat isolated words

They create sentences

A word list helps recognition. A pattern helps you answer.

They reduce fear

You already know the shape, so you only swap one word.

They invite correction

A tutor can improve your pronunciation and natural word choice quickly.
