School Morning

A short beginner reader for children: read the Roman Malayalam, check the English meaning, practise aloud, then use the quiz and AI tutor prompt.

Read the story aloud

Hear the whole story in Malayalam (voice: Meera)
Roman Malayalam
Ravile njan ezhunnelkkum.
Meaning
In the morning I wake up.
Roman Malayalam
Njan vellam kudikkum.
Meaning
I drink water.
Roman Malayalam
Amma choru tharum.
Meaning
Mother gives rice/food.
Roman Malayalam
Njan school-il pokum.
Meaning
I go to school.
Roman Malayalam
Teacher parayum: namaskaram.
Meaning
The teacher says: hello.

Practice goal: read once slowly, once naturally, then change one line to fit your own life.
