Proof standards

Traceable claims

Exact ratings, counts, awards, rankings, media and student results must have a source, date and review owner before public use.

Consent before stories

Student stories, screenshots, names, images and voices need explicit permission and manual review before publishing.

No instant-fluency promise

Malayalam progress depends on practice time, feedback, prior exposure, listening comfort and consistency.

Safe funnel claims

Payment, app-store, certification and audio/video claims stay blocked until their separate gates pass.

Outcome ladder

Week 1Pronounce key sounds, greet people and use survival phrases.
Month 1Handle simple family, food, travel and daily-life sentence patterns.
Month 3Hold guided conversations with prompts, correction and review.
OngoingBuild speed, listening comfort, vocabulary recall and cultural context.
