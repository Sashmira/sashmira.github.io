Ecosystem

Every asset feeds another asset

A family vocabulary lesson can become an article, video, worksheet, flashcard set, quiz, podcast, AI practice prompt, course lesson and book chapter. That is how SpeakMalayalam grows from pages into infrastructure.

  • Dictionary pages feed lessons and AI tutor prompts.
  • Lessons feed courses, worksheets and flashcards.
  • Courses feed certification readiness.
  • Certification feeds tutor and job trust.
  • Jobs and marketplace pages attract professionals and employers.
