The sweetest part: Malayali pet names

Whatever the official name, every Malayali child also gets a veetile peru (home name): Ammu, Appu, Achu, Unni, Kannan, Chinnu, Kichu. These affectionate names often stick for life — many adults answer to them at every family gathering. Learn more loving words on the family words page.

Naming traditions in Kerala

Traditionally a child may be named after a grandparent, after the family deity, or by the star (nakshatram) of birth — the 28th-day naming ceremony (noolukettu / namakaranam) is a treasured family event. Diaspora families often choose names that work in both worlds: easy for teachers abroad to say, rich with meaning at home.

Frequently asked questions

What are popular Malayalam baby girl names?

Classics include Anjali, Lakshmi, Meera, Nila, Kavya and Devika — names with meanings rooted in nature, poetry and tradition.

What are popular Malayalam baby boy names?

Arjun, Hari, Kannan, Madhav, Unni, Vishnu and Adithyan are beloved choices across Kerala and the diaspora.

What is a veetile peru?

The affectionate home name every Malayali child gets — like Ammu, Appu, Achu or Unni — often used by family for life regardless of the official name.

How are babies traditionally named in Kerala?

Often on the 28th day (noolukettu/namakaranam ceremony), choosing names linked to grandparents, the family deity, or the child\u2019s birth star (nakshatram).
