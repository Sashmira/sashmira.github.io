FAQ

Common questions

Is the AI Malayalam Tutor live today?

This is the foundation page for the future AI tutor. Current practice should use the lesson library, tools and human tutor support.

Why make an AI tutor for Malayalam?

Malayalam has fewer high-quality learning tools than larger languages, so teacher-approved AI practice can fill a major gap.

Will AI replace human lessons?

No. The strongest model is AI for repetition and humans for correction, culture, confidence and deeper guidance.

Use the human-led system now.

AI practice is strongest when it sits on top of clear lessons, real teacher correction and trusted examples.
