Malayalam Course Online: Speak Real Malayalam from Your First Lessons

A spoken-first Malayalam course for beginners, NRIs, partners, travellers, parents and adult learners who want useful conversation, not textbook memorisation.

This Malayalam course is built around one practical outcome: you should be able to understand and speak simple, natural Malayalam in real situations. The course uses romanised Malayalam, audio, sentence patterns, live correction and everyday topics before moving into deeper grammar or script.

Best fit: You want a structured Malayalam course, but you do not want to spend months memorising script before you can speak. Start with the free 30-day course, then move into live lessons when you want correction and accountability.

Suggested syllabus

Fluency practice, cultural confidence, roleplays, pronunciation correction and personalised speaking goals.

FAQ

Is this Malayalam course for absolute beginners?

Yes. It starts from simple sounds, greetings and useful sentence patterns. You do not need prior Malayalam knowledge.

Do I need to learn Malayalam script?

No, not at the beginning. The course can start with romanised Malayalam. Script becomes easier once you already know the sounds and common words.

Can I use this course with books?

Yes. The books, free lessons, audio tools and live classes should reinforce each other. One topic can become a lesson, worksheet, flashcard set, video and book chapter.
