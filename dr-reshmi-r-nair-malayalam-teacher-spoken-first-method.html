Reshmi R Nair Malayalam teacher: how her spoken-first method works

SpeakMalayalam is built around a simple teaching belief: beginners gain confidence when Malayalam is usable in real conversations from the start.

See teacher profile

The spoken-first method

Start with purpose. Learners connect Malayalam to family, Kerala travel, marriage, children, school, work or identity.
Build with patterns. Short reusable sentence shapes help students speak before grammar feels heavy.
Correct gently. Feedback focuses on sound, flow and natural daily phrasing.
Layer reading gradually. Script and grammar are added as confidence grows, not used as a wall at the beginning.

Who this helps

Total beginners

Students who know no Malayalam and need a friendly first path.

Heritage learners

Students who understand some Malayalam but freeze when speaking.

Families and kids

Parents who want Malayalam to feel usable at home, not like homework only.
